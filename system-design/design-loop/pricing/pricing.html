Diagnostic price sheet · cost-side source of truth

The priced test menu both the proposer (which tests to order, within budget) and the evaluator (what spend a design costs) hold as truth. The agent never sets prices; it selects from this sheet. Sourced from CMS fee schedules and vendor/list prices — real codes and rates, but approximate and not the clinic’s negotiated cost. Illustrative for now; verify before real use.

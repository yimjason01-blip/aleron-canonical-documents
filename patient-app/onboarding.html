Genetic testing is a separate decision.

Genetic results are permanent and can carry implications for your family. So this consent stands on its own, and it is entirely yours to give or decline.

What the panel looks at

Inherited cancer risk, cardiovascular genetics, and how your body processes common medications. Not a raw gene list. Your physician interprets everything in context.

How results are handled

Some findings are called pathogenic or likely pathogenic, which means the variant is known to raise risk. Any result that is serious, ambiguous, or needs clinical context is always held for a conversation with your physician first, regardless of the preference you choose below.

Your employer
